Prolog Practical – Shortest Solutions

1. Family Tree

parent(john, mary).
parent(mary, sam).

grandparent(X,Y):- parent(X,Z), parent(Z,Y).
sibling(X,Y):- parent(P,X), parent(P,Y), X\=Y.

2. conc(L1, L2, L3)

conc([],L,L).
conc([H|T],L,[H|R]):- conc(T,L,R).

3. reverse(L, R)

reverse([],[]).
reverse([H|T],R):- reverse(T,RT), append(RT,[H],R).

4. Sum of Two Numbers

sum(X,Y,Z):- Z is X+Y.

5. max(X, Y, M)

max(X,Y,X):- X>=Y.
max(X,Y,Y):- Y>X.

6. factorial(N, F)

fact(0,1).
fact(N,F):- N>0, N1 is N-1, fact(N1,F1), F is N*F1.

7. Fibonacci

fib(0,0).
fib(1,1).
fib(N,F):- N>1, N1 is N-1, N2 is N-2,
           fib(N1,F1), fib(N2,F2),
           F is F1+F2.

8. power(Num, Pow, Ans)

power(_,0,1).
power(N,P,R):- P>0, P1 is P-1, power(N,P1,R1), R is N*R1.

9. multi(N1, N2, R)

multi(X,Y,R):- R is X*Y.

10. memb(X, L)

memb(X,[X|_]).
memb(X,[_|T]):- memb(X,T).

11. sumlist(L, S)

sumlist([],0).
sumlist([H|T],S):- sumlist(T,S1), S is H+S1.

12. evenlength / oddlength

evenlength([]).
evenlength([_,_|T]):- evenlength(T).

oddlength([_]).
oddlength([_,_|T]):- oddlength(T).

13. maxlist(L, M)

maxlist([X],X).
maxlist([H|T],M):- maxlist(T,M1), M is max(H,M1).

14. insert(I, N, L, R)

insert(I,1,L,[I|L]).
insert(I,N,[H|T],[H|R]):- N>1, N1 is N-1, insert(I,N1,T,R).

15. delete(N, L, R)

delete(1,[_|T],T).
delete(N,[H|T],[H|R]):- N>1, N1 is N-1, delete(N1,T,R).
